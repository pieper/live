WebGPU Volume + Fiducials selftest

A port of the slicer-wgpu Volume + Fiducials selftest: markup control points rendered as solid procedural spheres — no geometry buffer, no texture, just an SDF test with plastic-Phong highlights evaluated inside the volume ray-march — composited with the DVR in a single front-to-back pass, so the pushpins occlude and are occluded by the tissue correctly. This is the same field nnLive click-points will use.  ·  SlicerLive
