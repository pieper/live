nnLive — interactive segmentation in the 4-up nnLive

The payoff: a real CT streamed into the SlicerLive 4-up, where each click runs interactive segmentation (nnLive's distilled nnInteractive model, an ONNX net evaluated on a 64³ patch on your GPU). The returned mask is splatted into a labelmap, re-baked with ColorizeVolume, and shown live as a colored 3D volume + MPR overlay, with a pushpin at each click. Click an organ in any MPR view; shift-click adds a background (negative) point to refine. The whole SlicerLive-side pipeline runs here; the nnLive model loads from its weights host (override with ?model=<url>). If it can't load, a built-in region-grow stub keeps the pipeline live (and says so).  ·  nnLive  ·  SlicerLive
