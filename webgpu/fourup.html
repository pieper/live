WebGPU 4-up — MPR + 3D preview

The Slicer-style 4-up, natively in WebGPU: three orthogonal MPR planes (windowed grayscale + colored segmentation overlay) and a 3D ColorizeVolume view — all from the same GPU textures via the field-based renderer.
