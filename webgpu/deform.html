WebGPU Landmark Deform — thin-plate spline selftest

A port of the slicer-wgpu Landmark Deform (TPS) selftest: a nonlinear transform authored from landmark pairs and applied during the ray march. The TransformField is a modifier — it contributes no colour; instead the volume samples through transform_point(), so the warp deforms the volume's apparent shape (and would equally move fiducials, a segmentation, or anything else that attaches it). Cyan pins are the source landmarks, magenta the targets. Drag the gain slider from identity to full warp.  ·  SlicerLive
