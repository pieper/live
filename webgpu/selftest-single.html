Single Volume — CTACardio selftest · real data

A port of the slicer-wgpu Single Volume selftest, on the same data it loads: CTACardio (512×512×321) streamed from the Jetstream2 bucket and ray-marched with the volume's own transfer function carried over from Slicer. Camera and mouse bindings match Slicer's vtkMRMLCameraWidget: drag = rotate, shift/middle = pan, right = zoom, wheel = dolly.  ·  SlicerLive
